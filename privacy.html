Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 Feb 2025

1. Introduction

UK Petitions ("the App") allows users to browse and track UK Government petitions. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled in the App.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • The App does not collect personal data from users or anyone else.
  • The App retrieves and re-publishes already publicly available petition data from the UK Government Petitions website.
  • No user tracking, analytics, or external data collection occurs.

3. Data Source

Petition data is retrieved from UK Government Petitions.

All content is used under the Open Government Licence v3.0.

For any concerns, please visit the Support Page.
